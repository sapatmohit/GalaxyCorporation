Welcome To Galaxy Corporation

Galaxy Corporation is one of the leading Exporter & Trader for Agro Commodities in India. The company is exporting agro commodities to customers across the globe. We are located in Pune which is a major business hub for agro commodities. The company deals in a wide range of products such as Oil Seeds, Cereals, Spices, Fresh Vegetable, Fresh Fruits, Frozen Vegetable, Frozen Fruits, Dry Fruits, Pulses, etc.

The core strength of the Company is its strong procurement network, storage, processing, and supply of all agricultural commodities which extends into all important producing centers across India. We have the necessary expertise and backbone to meet any scale of demand for agricultural commodities.

    Welcome To Galaxy Corporation

    Galaxy Corporation is one of the leading Exporter & Trader for Agro Commodities in India. The company is exporting agro commodities to customers across the globe. We are located in Pune which is a major business hub for agro commodities. The company deals in a wide range of products such as Oil Seeds, Cereals, Spices, Fresh Vegetable, Fresh Fruits, Frozen Vegetable, Frozen Fruits, Dry Fruits, Pulses, etc.

    Galaxy Corporation Agro has its vision to improving the quality of life of the rural population, including those who are resource poor, by widening their employment opportunities and increasing their income. The vision seeks to accelerate demand driven agricultural and agro industrial growth in the new globalized agricultural environment.

    A 'no-compromise-on-quality' approach in all production processes and no 'allowance-for-shortcomings' in all business dealings, together with a total commitment to continuous improvement and consumer satisfaction at all levels, to achieve and maintain a frontline position among similar ventures is our mission.

    We would like to introduce ourselves as Galaxy Corporation is one of the leading agro Exporter & Trader, The Company is a permanent member of Indo-Arab Chamber of Commerce, Mahratta Chamber of Commerce Industries and Agriculture, APEDA, IOPEPC, MSAMB and Spices Board.

    We are operating our offices from Mumbai & Pune . The main activity is export of all types of Agricultural commodities to various countries all over the globe.

    The core strength of the Company is its strong procurement network, storage, processing, and supply of all agricultural commodities which extends into all important producing centers across India. We have the necessary expertise and backbone to meet any scale of demand for agricultural commodities.
